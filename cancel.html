Need to cancel your purchase? Our streamlined process ensures your request is handled quickly and transparently.

Cancellation Terms

We understand that plans can change. To ensure a fair process for both buyers and our developers, please follow these guidelines:

  • Log in to your Dashboard and navigate to "My Purchases".
  • Locate the order you wish to cancel and click Cancel Request.
  • Wait for our support team to verify the status of your project delivery.

Important Notes

  • Digital products already downloaded are not eligible for cancellation.
  • Custom builds can only be cancelled within 12 hours of the initial request.
  • Refunds for cancelled orders will be processed back to your original payment method.
