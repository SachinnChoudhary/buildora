Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information at Buildora Marketplace.

Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum amount of data required to provide our students and developers with a premium marketplace experience.

  • Account information (name, email, profile picture).
  • Transaction details (purchase history, order status).
  • Technical details (IP address, browser type, device information).

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to personalize your experience and ensure secure transactions.

  • Processing payments and delivering digital assets securely.
  • Providing technical support for project implementation.
  • Sending critical updates about your orders and requests.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences. You can manage cookie settings through your browser at any time.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with trusted partners (like payment processors) necessary to complete your transactions.
