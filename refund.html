Refund Policy

Transparent and fair refund process for all Buildora digital projects and custom development services.

Our Commitment

We strive for 100% satisfaction. Since we deal with digital goods, our refund policy is designed to be fair to both students and our developers.

Eligibility for Refund

  • Major defects in the project code that prevent it from running as described.
  • Files missing from the download package (documentation, reports, etc.).
  • Failure to deliver custom projects within the agreed-upon timeline.

Refund Process

  • Submit a support ticket within 48 hours of purchase.
  • Our technical team will verify the issue and attempt to fix it within 24 hours.
  • If the issue persists, a full or partial refund will be processed immediately.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Projects that have been significantly modified by the purchaser.
  • Purchases made more than 7 days ago.
  • Incompatibility with software versions not specified in the project description.
